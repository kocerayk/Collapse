Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

When will my shipment be delivered?

You can find out the delivery date through the SMS sent to you when the delivery is received.

How can I track the status of my shipment?

You can check the status by entering the tracking number on our website.

On which days and during what hours do you make deliveries?

Deliveries are made between 09:00 and 21:00 on weekdays and Saturdays.

What should I pay attention to during delivery?

Check the outer packaging of your shipment for any damage before accepting it.

Can someone else accept the delivery on my behalf?

Yes, a different person at your address can accept the delivery with a TC ID number and signature.

Can I change the delivery address after placing an order?

Yes, you can update the delivery address before the shipment is dispatched by contacting our customer service.

What should I do if my shipment is delayed?

If your shipment is delayed, please contact our customer support for assistance and tracking updates.
